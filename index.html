The Viewer Experience

FlipFeeds bridges the gap between interactive web gaming and the creator economy. It operates like an infinite digital World Expo, where players explore dynamic, top-down pixel environments known as Feeds.

When a Feed experiences a Desync—like a corrupted industrial 3D printer violently extruding static in the Maker Feed—players must step outside the game. They hunt for clues hidden within actual CodingCat.dev YouTube broadcasts to find the continuity override keys.

// THE GAMEPLAY LOOP

  • Explore the Feeds: Navigate massively multiplayer public zones.
  • Spot the Desync: Identify narrative and visual glitches.
  • The Research Phase: Scour YouTube archives for the continuity key.
  • The Edit Bay: Retreat to private, speakeasy-style craft bourbon lounges to strategize with your guild.
  • The Flip: Enter the key, clear the static, and restore the broadcast.

Edge-Native Architecture

// SUB-50MS MULTIPLAYER

We are replacing traditional, expensive monolithic game servers with localized edge computing. By leveraging Cloudflare's global network, FlipFeeds acts as a high-visibility showcase for the future of web applications.

Public Feeds and private Edit Bays are spun up on-demand as Durable Objects, handling thousands of concurrent WebSocket connections natively at the edge.

Built by a team rooted in enterprise solutions engineering and developer education, the platform is designed to scale infinitely. New "Seasons" of content are deployed without interrupting the live game state, utilizing modern React UI overlays seamlessly layered over a high-performance WebGL canvas.
